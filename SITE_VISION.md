# SITE_VISION.md: Fråga Saga - The Authority on Swedish Quotes

This document outlines the vision for fragasaga.se to become the undisputed #1 resource in Sweden for quote and invoice verification. We are not just a blog; we are a data-driven utility.

## The Vision

Fråga Saga will be the go-to platform for any Swede, from homeowner to small business owner, who feels uncertain about a quote or invoice from a service provider. Our brand will be synonymous with trust, transparency, and empowerment.

We will achieve this by excelling in three core areas:

1.  **UÖVERTRÄFFADE VERKTYG (Unmatched Tools):** Free, instant, and accurate AI-powered tools to analyze quotes, compare offers, and verify invoices.
2.  **AUKTORITativt INNEHÅLL (Authoritative Content):** The most comprehensive and practical guides on everything related to quotes, invoices, consumer rights (ROT/RUT), and company verification (F-skatt).
3.  **UNIK DATA-INSIKT (Unique Data Insights):** We will become the primary source for data on service pricing in Sweden, publishing original reports that are cited by media, government, and the industry itself.

## The Data Moat

Our long-term competitive advantage (our "moat") is the data we collect. Every analysis feeds our dataset, making our tools and insights progressively better and harder to replicate.

-   **Phase 1 (Current):** Capture every analysis (anonymously) into a structured database.
-   **Phase 2:** Enhance tools to capture more structured data (e.g., line items, materials vs. labor).
-   **Phase 3:** Publish the first "Saga Index" - a report on average pricing for common services, based on our proprietary data.
